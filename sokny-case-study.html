Project Overview
What is Sokny?

The App

Sokny is a general real estate marketplace designed for the Egyptian market. It connects tenants, buyers, and property owners through an experience built on trust, transparency, and user rights — not just listings.

The Mission

To build a platform where both renters and owners feel protected and respected. Sokny puts user needs first — offering verified listings, in-app contracts, and privacy-first communication so no one's personal data is ever exposed.

The Challenge
Problem & Solution

😤 The Problem

Existing Egyptian real estate apps were built with owners in mind — not users. Listings were unverified, important features were missing, and renters had zero protection. Worse, sharing your phone number meant risking your privacy with strangers.

✅ The Solution

Sokny flips the script. Every listing is verified. Contracts are signed digitally inside the app. Users chat directly with agents without sharing their number. It's real estate done right — for everyone.

Research
UX Research Process
01

Stakeholder Interviews

Spoke with property owners, agents, and renters to understand the full ecosystem and each party's needs.

02

User Interviews & Surveys

Conducted structured interviews and distributed surveys to gather quantitative and qualitative insights from real users.

03

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed existing Egyptian real estate platforms — mapping gaps, missing features, and unmet user expectations.

04

WHM + Brainstorming

Used Why-How-Map and collaborative brainstorming to define the best features and design solutions for each persona.

Research Output
User Personas
🧑‍🎓
The Renter

A young professional or student searching for a safe, fairly priced apartment — frustrated by unverified listings and privacy violations.

🏠
The Owner

A property owner who wants maximum visibility, reliable tenants, and a secure way to manage contracts without paperwork.

💼
The Buyer

A first-time buyer looking to purchase property — needs detailed info, map context, and verified agent communication.

Key Findings
Pain Points Found
🔒

Privacy Violation

Users were forced to share phone numbers to inquire about listings — exposing them to unsolicited calls and data misuse.

⚖️

Owner Bias

Existing platforms clearly favored property owners, leaving renters with little protection, no verification, and no legal safety net.

🗺️

Missing Features

No map context, weak filters, no in-app contracts, and no way to check the credibility of listings or agents.

Design Solutions
Key Features Designed
📝

In-App Contracts

Sign rental/purchase contracts digitally inside the app — protecting both tenant and owner legally.

🗺️

Interactive Map

Visual map showing listings and nearby facilities — schools, mosques, malls, transit — for informed decisions.

💬

Direct In-App Chat

Contact agents directly through the app — no phone number sharing, full privacy guaranteed.

🔍

Smart Filters

Rich filtering tailored to each user type — rent, buy, near me, student housing — with precise results.

✅

Verified Listings

Every listing goes through a verification process so users can trust what they see before they visit.

👤

Role-Based Onboarding

Users choose their role — Tenant, Buyer, or Owner — and get a tailored experience from day one.

UI Design
App Screens

The UI was designed in Figma with a clean, trustworthy aesthetic. Arabic-first layout, accessible typography, and a calming green palette convey safety and reliability — values at Sokny's core.
